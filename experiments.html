Why MathML Was Not Commonly Used Before

Although MathML has existed in the HTML specification for decades, it was not widely adopted due to inconsistent browser support. Until recently, only Firefox had native MathML rendering. Chrome and Safari lacked proper implementation, making developers rely on images or libraries like MathJax.

In 2023 and beyond, browser vendors began integrating native MathML support, improving accessibility and performance. As a result, MathML is now practical for web use. (Source: Can I Use â€“ MathML)
