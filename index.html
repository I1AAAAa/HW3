Welcome to the Jungian Cognitive Type Test

This website is inspired by Carl Jung’s theory of psychological types, which describes how people perceive the world and make decisions through eight fundamental cognitive functions Thinking, Feeling, Sensing, and Intuition — each expressed in either an introverted or extraverted form.

About Carl Jung's Cognitive Function Theory

This website provides a simplified exploration of Carl Jung’s cognitive function theory. Carl Jung proposed that everyone uses eight mental processes — combinations of Thinking, Feeling, Sensing, and Intuition — each expressed in either an introverted or extraverted form. These functions describe how we perceive information and make decisions. Discovering your dominant patterns can deepen your self-understanding and highlight your natural strengths.
